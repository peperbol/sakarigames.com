ABOUT US

Sakari Games is a Games Development Studio based in Antwerp, Belgium. For over 10 years Sakari has been delivering games for a diversity of platforms including Nintendo Switch, Xbox, VR titles and mobile games. The talented team is ready to help you with all your digital needs from UX, Programming, Video Game Ports, 3D Artwork, Animation, Rigging, Texture Art, to Marketing Assets.

projects

TRAIL OF NANOOK

Trail of Nanook is currently under development for the Nintendo Switch. The game is a cooperative adventure that takes place on the arctic.

Estimated releasedate: 2020

FOREIGN LEGION: BUCKETS OF BLOOD

Foreign Legion: Buckets of blood was released in 2008 and was the first Belgium title to be released on Steam. It is a cartoony, gory, singleplayer shooter that does not take itself too serious

It is available for download on Steam.

TRAMPOLINE PARK STARS

Trampoline Park stars is an unique crossover between video games and marketing. Players can win real life prizes in the game and collect them at Trampoline parks. It creates a loop between the franchizes and its customers.

The game is also being inhertited by the INTERNATIONAL ASSOCIATION OF TRAMPOLINE PARKS

FOREIGN LEGION: MULTI MASSACRE

The FOREIGN LEGION Series continue with this multiplayer cartoon slaughter fest. Team up against zombies and dangerous dolphines.

The game is available for download on Steam.

CARGO BREACH

Cargo Breach is not for the faint hearted as this VR shooter will drop you on a gritty space ship with scary monsters crawling in the dark. A unique weapon switching system will help you shred them to juicy bits.

The game is available for download on Steam.

TRAILER TRASHERS

Trailer Trashers. A hectic bullet bouncing party game for Nintendo Switch, Xbox One and Steam. The game can be wishlisted on steam right now.

Estimated releasedate: Early 2020

ROVER WARS

Team with up to 8 friends on one screen in this fast paced battle arena. Use your Rover to build an army with a variety of units. Bash the opposing forces or call in a devastating death-beam. Expand your build area by building factories and achieve total supremacy!
